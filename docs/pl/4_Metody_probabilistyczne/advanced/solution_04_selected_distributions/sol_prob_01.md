# Problem 1 — Coin × Coin

## Sample Space

The experiment consists of two coin tosses.

Ω = {(H,H), (H,T), (T,H), (T,T)}

---

## Part A — Events

**1. Exactly one head**

{(H,T), (T,H)}

---

**2. Both tosses are the same**

{(H,H), (T,T)}

---

**3. At least one head**

{(H,H), (H,T), (T,H)}

---

**4. The first toss is tails**

{(T,H), (T,T)}

---

**5. The second toss is heads**

{(H,H), (T,H)}

---

## Part B — Interpretation

**Case 1**

Outcomes: {(H,H), (H,T)}

Interpretation:  
The first toss is heads.

---

**Case 2**

Outcomes: {(H,T), (T,H)}

Interpretation:  
Exactly one head occurs.
